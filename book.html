Logan watched, his eyes intense as they tracked her every move. She interacted with the chat, pasting on the same fake smile—the very one he knew he would ruin later. Her name was Katlin, but to her online followers, she was "KatetheLate."

"Never late to being a predator, huh?" Logan muttered to himself.

At 11:08, Katlin finally said, "Welp, it seems my time's up."

"It sure is," Logan murmured. He started to pack his equipment, ready to kill her.

He got in his car and set his GPS to the location he had found. The address came from someone who had doxxed her and proven she was a child predator.

He arrived at her house and knocked.

"Who are you?" Katlin asked.

"I'm consequences," Logan replied.

He lunged with a bag he had kept hidden, slipping behind her in one swift move. He held the bag tight, cutting off her oxygen and knocking her out.

Logan caught her body, looking at her with disgust. He put her unconscious body in her car and started the engine to make his way back to his "fishershop." Underneath this shop, he had a wall with cuffs ready for his next victim. He set her body up carefully so as not to wake her up. He then kicked her body to wake her up. "Hey, Katlin," Logan said. "Have you ever heard that saying? It is something like, 'You reap what you sow'?"

"I DID NOTHING!" screamed Katlin. "LET ME GO, YOU PSYCHOPATH!" She screamed again.

"You know I would believe you if I didn't have evidence," Logan said.

"What evidence?" Katlin said under her breath.

"The evidence of you trapping and raping that 17-year-old kid. You're 24, sicko," said Logan.

"Okay, get it over with. Arrest me," said Katlin.

"Oh, sweetie," Logan chuckled. "You've misunderstood this situation. I'm not a police officer. Like I said before, 'I'm consequence.'"

"WHAT THE HELL DOES THAT MEAN?" Katlin screamed.

"It means I'm here to give you what you deserve. You don't deserve to live in a jail for the rest of your life. Hell, for that matter, you don't deserve to live at all." Said Logan

Her heart pounded, a drum against her ribs, as Logan walked over to his arrangement of deadly weapons. He paused, running a finger along the cold steel, then proceeded to ask, his voice silk and terrifying "Which one shall I use to end your filthy existence?"

Logan pulled a cloth off an object, revealing a wheel marked with three weapons: the blunt Hammer, the focused Switchblade, and the taut Garrote. He held it up for her to see. "See, Katlin, I'm not a judge, I'm truly an instrument."

He spun the wheel and, after a while of spinning, the pointer settled on the Garrote. Logan accepted the choice with a chilling chuckle and nod, grabbing it.

"You reaped a child's innocence, and now, by divine degree, you shall reap consequence."
