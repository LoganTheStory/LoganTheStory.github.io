Logan: Reap & Sow

A THRILLER

Logan watched, his eyes intense as they tracked her every move. She interacted with the chat, pasting on the same fake smile—the very one he knew he would ruin later. Her name was Katlin, but to her online followers, she was "KatetheLate."

"Never late to being a predator, huh?" Logan muttered to himself.

At 11:08, Katlin finally said, "Welp, it seems my time's out."

"It sure is," Logan murmured to himself. He started to pack his equipment, ready to kill her.

He got in his car and set his GPS to the location he had found. The address came from someone who had doxxed her and proved she was a child predator.

He arrived at her house and knocked.

"Who are you?" Katlin asked.

"I'm consequences," Logan replied.

He lunged with a bag he had kept hidden, slipping behind her in one swift move. He held the bag tight, cutting off her oxygen and knocking her out.
